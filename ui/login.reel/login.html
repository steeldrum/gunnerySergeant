The Gunnery Sergeant Organization

GunnerySergeant.org - a groundswell regulated militia organization

After reading the second amendment: "A well regulated Militia, being necessary to the security of a free State, the right of the people to keep and bear Arms", we decided to take a stab at establishing a "a groundswell regulated militia organization!"

Our vision is simple: create a groundswell of public opinion to promote sane, common sense militia regulations (with benefits to gun owners and no infringement of KABA Rights).

Currently in "beta" mode, when completed, this web site is to provide these features:

  • A "Gunnery Sergeant" role (mentor/big brother or sister) to help and advise "newbies" (new gun owners)
  • Once funds are provided (donations to a nonprofit), a program to supply "newbies" a cash rebate to defer the cost of insuring against loss or theft of his or her gun.
  • A membership card issued to "newbies" (upon request) that identifies them as a proud member of a 21st century militia organization!

Are you "loaded" with questions about us? Feel free to "Fire Away"! 

You're certainly able to "fire away" with questions. We'll try to be an easy "target". But we assume that you are (or maybe plan to) be a responsible gun owner. Safety comes first. The safest option is to read all the FAQs (below) before asking your question!

FAQs About Us

  • What does "currently in beta mode" mean? It means this web site is under construction. Collaborators use it for test purposes. Any data you enter could be lost!
  • Who are the collaborators? They are a volunteer corp who communicate using Collogistics (We can be reached using that web site, our host-providers and sponsors.)
  • Who is the so-called "Gunnery Sergeant"? When folks join up (see home page) they are asked (via a simple check box option) if they intend to help newbies: fulfill the GS role - a mentor/big brother or sister helping new gun owners.
  • If I join as a "newbie" (not a "Gunnery Sergeant") what happens? If you provide a means to receive it, you'll get a membership card (and related details - for example about how to apply for your gun insurance cost partial rebate). A GS may contact you as well.
  • What do you mean when you say "once funds are provided (donations to a nonprofit)"? We are just beginning the process of setting up this site as a nonprofit. Presently we cannot accept donations. In the future we hope the donations can be used to defray our costs as well as fund our insurance rebate initiative.
  • None of the above questions have answered my question. What can I do? We suggest visiting Collogistics where they have several 'wikis' related to assorted projects. The so-called "OddBulb" (weird ideas) wiki has specifics. Collogistics can be emailed and will forward GS questions to us!
  • Anything else? We solicit beta site feedback and/or questions: we can be emailed via gunnerySergeant@collogistics.com
