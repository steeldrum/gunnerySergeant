We solicit volunteer Sponsors!

Understand the role a Sponsor plays. If the application is approved then details will be emailed to the Sponsor collaborator. However a summary is here:

  • The Sponsor is a trusted friend or acquaintance of a gun owner (private or GS).
  • The Sponsor may be emailed (triggerred by a GS log request) when the gun owner is an uncertified private.
  • The Sponsor is expected to maintain confidentiality regarding the gun owner's information except for that person's handle.
  • The Sponsor is expected to be accredited by the Gunnery Sergeant Organization.
  • The Sponsor is expected to take appropriate action if concerns arise regarding the health, well-being or intentions of the gun owner. The actions never include contacting this organization. Typically they would involve confronting the gun owner and/or discussing issues with the owner's friends or appropriate authorities.
  • The Sponsor may be a relative of the gun owner.
  • The Sponsor may initiate the enlistment of the gun owner but only as an uncertified private. In that case the Sponsor has the obligation to inform the owner about the credentials used to enlist, and suggest that the enlistee adjust his or her profile (perhaps becomming certified, for example).
  • The Sponsor candidate application will always be rejected if the Sponsor applicant is a private or a GS who owns a gun.

In many cases, prospective Sponsors may have already enlisted as booster member collaborators. If so your profile will change assigning the new role as a sponsor!

Once the organization acknowledges you as a sponsor, a private gun owner may make a formal request to make you a sponsor (via enlistment as a private).
