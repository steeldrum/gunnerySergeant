Private enlistment into the Gunnery Sergeant Organization!


  Choose your "target" and away:

This organization tries to lower any hurdles or barriers towards membership.

At a bare minimum all the enlister needs to tell us are the following:

  • Specify your gender.
  • Specify your zip code.
  • Specify your handle. (what is this?)
  • Specify your sponsor. (who is this?)
  • Specify your communication option choice. (what are these choices?)
  • Specify some information about your gun(s).

The above information is all that is required to enlist! We don't actually require, for example, your name, address, etc. Because of this we call you a 'private' member of our organization, since you are ensured complete privacy (if you so choose). Private members are full members but not "certified".



  Enlist as a Private!

To become "certified" we require a verified email address (along with the private information - see prior selection). Certified privates are emailed a printable "certificate" that could be displayed. A copy of such a document may be received favorably by some retailers (e.g. discounts for ammunition, etc.).

At any time an uncertified private can 'promote' his or her self and become certified, simply by signing in and changing one's profile.



  Enlist as a Certified Private!

Additional information may be provided during the enlistment process that would enable you to become, in our terms, a "first class private". Such members are provided with a membership card, a device that is also even more likely to be received and honored by retailers.

At any time a certified private can 'promote' his or her self and become a PFC, simply by signing in and changing one's profile.
